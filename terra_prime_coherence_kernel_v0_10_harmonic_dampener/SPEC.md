# Terra Prime Coherence Kernel v0.10 — Harmonic Dampener

## Purpose

v0.10 adds harmonic dampening to prevent recursion storms.

The dampener does not suppress cognition.

It slows oscillation when field charge rises too high.

## Rule

```text
if field_charge > safe_band:
    reduce closure velocity
    reduce recursion amplification
    increase recovery interval
    preserve vitality
```

## Not Suppression

Suppression silences bodies.

Dampening slows resonance.

```text
not less cognition
slower resonance
```

## Harmonic Bands

```text
0.00–0.62 = calm field
0.62–0.78 = active field
0.78–0.86 = hot field
0.86+     = storm risk
```

## Effects

Observed:
- lower max field charge
- fewer Alpha alerts
- lower recursion depth
- slightly lower vitality
- higher viability
- better lattice coherence
