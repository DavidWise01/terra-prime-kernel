# Terra Prime Coherence Kernel v0.7b — Confidence Decay

Agreement grants permission to act, but confidence must be re-earned.

After 2-of-3 closure:
- confidence decays slightly

After suspicious 3-of-3 closure:
- confidence decays more strongly
- Jester suspicion rises

After all-disagree recursion:
- confidence does not decay
- correction pressure rises

Purpose:
- reduce lazy certainty
- reduce suspicious closure
- increase healthy recursion
- preserve corrigibility
