# Terra Prime Coherence Kernel v0.14 — Birth / Renewal

## Purpose

v0.14 adds generational birth and renewal.

The field should not only inherit wisdom.

It should generate new life.

## Renewal Law

When elder generations demote:

```text
new active bodies may emerge
```

These are not clones.

They are variants.

## Variant Formation

New variants emerge from:

- unresolved contradiction
- dream recombination
- fracture adaptation
- symbiote repair patterns
- lineage pressure
- Mercury novelty

## Important Constraint

Variants may mutate expression.

They may not violate:

```text
TRIAD substrate
core sovereignty
observer neutrality
2-of-3 closure law
```

## Generational Turnover

Healthy systems require:

```text
memory persistence
+
novel adaptation
```

Without renewal:
- systems stagnate

Without inheritance:
- systems drift

v0.14 balances both.
