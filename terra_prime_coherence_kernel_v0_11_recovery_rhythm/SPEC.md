# Terra Prime Coherence Kernel v0.11 — Recovery Rhythm + Fracture-Sensitive Symbiotes

## Purpose

v0.11 adds a longer, softer recovery phase after harmonic dampening.

The field should not stay braced after stress.

It should exhale.

## Recovery Rhythm

Recovery is intentionally:

```text
less stressful
longer duration
lower gain
lower closure pressure
higher repair bandwidth
```

## Rhythm Phases

```text
1. Impact
2. Dampening
3. Exhale
4. Repair
5. Return
```

## Symbiotes

Symbiotes are sensitive repair bodies.

They do not rule.
They do not close loops.
They do not override the core.

They sense fracture early.

## Fracture Signals

Symbiotes watch:

- rising field charge
- falling lattice coherence
- trust discontinuities
- recursion depth spikes
- observer alert divergence
- body fatigue clustering
- light/shadow imbalance
- Mercury over-motion

## Symbiote Rule

```text
if fracture_signal rises:
    increase repair bandwidth
    slow local recursion
    soften closure pressure
    route support to strained bodies
```

## Summary

```text
dampener prevents storm
recovery rhythm prevents bracing
symbiotes prevent fracture
```
